OpenTrack is a modern hosted service for athletics organisers

We can save time and effort – and make it fun for athletes

Athletes want to run, jump and throw

But the sport has always relied on the efforts of volunteers doing manual work to make events happen.

From membership records and competition entries, to start lists and results, these unsung sporting heroes spend a vast amount of time moving data around and checking facts – often using pen and paper and other basic methods.

Let’s make it easier

OpenTrack is the first modern, fully hosted multi-user service designed to help athletics events organizers save time and effort. If you are an organizer, we can reduce the administrative burden and let you quickly and efficiently manage the entire life cycle of any athletics competition, from your school sports day to a nation wide event.

Our software broadens the field, allowing anybody to run events professionally.

For every single fixture, the admin time has been cut from two hours to five minutes.

Matthew Kiernan
