OPENTRACK

Making athletics go faster.

Entries

Entries for track and field, cross country, road and relays. By individuals or teams.

Timing/Recording

Manage races the same way as top officials, using a handful of mobile devices.

Results

Publish your results. Validated. Real time. Syndicated. Searchable. All formats and devices.

An information strategy for the sport...

Everyone wants to run, jump and throw. Yet the sport is limited by a number of volunteers. They spend far too much time moving around facts and checking them - entries, start lists, membership info. We can reduce this burden, and let them spend the time doing more valuable things - coaching, and running events

Athletes will enjoy things more when they can follow results quickly and easily

Open Data

Building the open reference data to get everyone talking: organisations, clubs, venues, courses

Open Standards

Defining standard formas for interchange of entries, start lists, results and rankings

Open Source

If we start to publish data and standards, other people can build on it
